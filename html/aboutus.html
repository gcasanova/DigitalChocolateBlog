About Us

Digital Chocolate is a leader in social games across rapidly growing social networks and mobile platforms, and is best known for games including Galaxy Life, Millionaire City, Tower Bloxx, Zombie Lane, Army Attack, Crazy Penguin, and Rollercoaster Rush.


In regards to our studio in Barcelona, we are a medium sized group of young and really enthusiastic professionals, our job is our passion. We enjoy complete flexibility and freedom to make decisions regarding our projects, so let us know if you think you have the idea of the century.


Hopefully we will be able to show you a little of what happens in the backstage of a videogames studio on a daily basis through this site and let everybody know the incredible work carried out in our facilities.
